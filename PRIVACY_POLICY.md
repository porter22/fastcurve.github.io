# Privacy Policy for DCR Tracker

**Last Updated:** June 2026

## 1. Introduction

DCR Tracker ("the app") is a weight tracking and health monitoring application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

## 2. Data Collection

We collect the following health and personal information:
- Email address (for authentication)
- Weight measurements
- Height
- Gender
- Waist measurements
- Calculated BMI values
- Target weight and dates

All data is collected only with your explicit consent when you create an account or log measurements.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:
- Create and maintain your user account
- Calculate your weight loss curve and pace
- Display your health tracking history
- Generate personalized insights (target dates, weekly pace, BMI calculations)

We do **not**:
- Sell or share your data with third parties
- Use your data for marketing or advertising
- Share your data outside the EU (data is stored in EU region)

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase (AWS EU West 1)
- Communication between your device and our servers uses industry-standard HTTPS encryption
- Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text
- Each user can only access their own data (enforced via Row-Level Security)

## 5. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app's Settings menu:
1. Open Settings (gear icon)
2. Select "Delete account"
3. Confirm deletion

Your data will be permanently removed from our servers within seconds of deletion.

## 6. User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your personal data
- Request a copy of your data
- Delete your account and data at any time
- Request corrections to inaccurate data

To exercise these rights, delete your account through the app or contact us (see below).

## 7. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase (https://supabase.com) for:
- User authentication
- Database hosting
- Data storage and encryption

Supabase's privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

## 8. Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- When you delete your account, all data is permanently removed
- We do not maintain backups of deleted user data

## 9. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
